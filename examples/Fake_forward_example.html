We hope you will join us at Homecoming on Saturday, Oct. 11, as we celebrate Flash’s Birthday Bash with this year’s Homecoming theme. Since 1985, Flash has been a pillar of Kent State spirit. This year, we proudly celebrate the birthday of our beloved mascot Flash!
