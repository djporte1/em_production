Join CAVS at Our Party Tent!

The Center for Adult and Veteran Services (CAVS) invites you to stop by the party tent prior to and during the Military Appreciation Game where Kent State will take on Bowling Green.

The party will kick off at Dix Stadium, followed by the football game. Meet us on the northeast side of the stadium, near the student section and closed end zone. You will have the opportunity to enjoy food and connect with other veteran alumni and staff, plus current veteran students before the game. Game tickets are required to enter the stadium and are not included with your registration.

When: Saturday, Oct. 25 | Time: TBD*
Where: Dix Stadium - Northeast side of the stadium
2213 Summit St., Kent, OH 44242
*Due to TV scheduling, the game time will be announced 10 days prior to the game.

RSVP

You can purchase your game tickets - at a discounted rate - separately through the Kent State Athletics website.
